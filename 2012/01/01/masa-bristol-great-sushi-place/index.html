Masa Bristol - A great sushi place

Published on: 01 Sunday January 2012 at 04 o'clock in the evening. This post is about: bristol, and food.

Happy New Year to all.

To celebrate, I’ve just eaten some excellent sushi at Masa in Bristol. Really nice and cozy and some wonderful cooking too!

We had: snow crab dumplings, yellow tail and jalapeños sashimi, a bento box, tempura. We finished with a cheesecake (it is new year’s day!) and drank traditional Japanese beverages: Whiskey and beer. I recommend it thoroughly if you are in the area.
