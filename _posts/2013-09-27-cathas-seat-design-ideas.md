<div style="text-align: justify;">A good friend of mine, <a href="http://nickandscottgobiking.blogspot.co.uk/" target="_blank">Nick</a>,&nbsp; is organising the construction of a bicycle seat on route 18 of the Cycle Network to complete the work started by his mother, Catha. You can read more about it on the <a href="http://www.cathas-seat.org/" target="_blank">Catha's Seat</a> website.</div><div style="text-align: justify;">
</div>

<!-- more -->

<div style="text-align: justify;">As part of this I thought it would be cool to have an Android app to support the project. Firstly to raise awareness and guide users to the bike seat and secondly because it's fun!</div><div style="text-align: justify;">
</div><div style="text-align: justify;">I have put together some design ideas to support the building of the app and have already <a href="https://github.com/mezpahlan/CathasSeat" target="_blank">started coding</a> an early version. It's all open source if you want to help out so feel free to let me know if you're interested.</div><div style="text-align: justify;">
</div><div style="text-align: justify;">Here's the design I have so far.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-awaYrWb1HRg/UkVWEzc1-CI/AAAAAAAAYVQ/igmHncU8Nx8/s1600/start_activity.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-awaYrWb1HRg/UkVWEzc1-CI/AAAAAAAAYVQ/igmHncU8Nx8/s1600/start_activity.png" height="320" width="264" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">The app will start on a welcome page with an image of Catha's Seat in the surrounding area and some welcome text.</div><div style="text-align: justify;">
</div><div style="text-align: justify;">1) The user will enter a starting location to receive directions to the seat. In later versions I hope to make use of the GPS for this as an option.</div><div style="text-align: justify;">
</div><div style="text-align: justify;">2) Pressing the Go button takes you to the next screen - the map.</div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-VNOktsfYJUo/UkVWIvHPNDI/AAAAAAAAYVk/bxBockvGiRo/s1600/map_activity.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-VNOktsfYJUo/UkVWIvHPNDI/AAAAAAAAYVk/bxBockvGiRo/s1600/map_activity.png" height="320" width="278" /></a></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">The next screen is a Google Map with a directions plotted as a route.</div><div style="text-align: justify;">
</div><div style="text-align: justify;">3) The route will lead to a marker for Catha's Seat.</div>

<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ZtrODGttbL0/UkVWImK13II/AAAAAAAAYVY/cbvGaoN1sqE/s1600/directions_activity.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ZtrODGttbL0/UkVWImK13II/AAAAAAAAYVY/cbvGaoN1sqE/s1600/directions_activity.png" height="320" width="279" /></a></div>


<div style="text-align: justify;">Swiping to the right brings you to a screen with text directions</div><div style="text-align: justify;">
</div><div style="text-align: justify;">4) Text directions will appear as a list from the Google Maps query.
</div><div style="text-align: justify;">5) Additional information (if any) about the selected direction will appear here.
&nbsp; </div><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"></div><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"></div><div style="text-align: justify;">The user can interact with the different app screens by swiping left and right any time after an initial query has been performed.</div>

<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-28Ju__4yFhU/UkVWIjjCREI/AAAAAAAAYVc/mHYz5PZhGbs/s1600/interaction.png" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-28Ju__4yFhU/UkVWIjjCREI/AAAAAAAAYVc/mHYz5PZhGbs/s1600/interaction.png" height="216" width="640" /></a></div><div style="text-align: justify;">So that is the basic idea. Other ideas will be to share a picture of the user at Catha's Seat and an about page with more information about Catha's Seat. I am also toying with the idea of a donations button but don't really know how that will work.</div>
